How It Works
Complaint Workflow

End-to-end grievance management with secure citizen confirmation.

01
Citizen Initiates Complaint

Citizen sends a WhatsApp message to the TVK bot number to begin complaint registration.

02
OTP Verification

System validates the mobile number using a 6-digit OTP authentication.

03
Identity Collection

Aadhaar, Voter ID, Ward Number, and complaint details are securely captured.

04
AI Summary & Confirmation

Complaint is AI-summarized and submitted only after citizen taps OK to approve.

05
Admin Allocation

Constituency admin receives notification with severity score and assigns to right team.

06
Resolution Closure

Final OTP confirmation from citizen officially closes and archives the complaint.
